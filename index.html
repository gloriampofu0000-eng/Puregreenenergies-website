Our Mission

To provide an affordable, sustainable, and eco-friendly diesel alternative that reduces carbon emissions while supporting industries that rely on diesel-powered equipment.

Our Vision

To become a leading biodiesel supplier across Africa, particularly in regions facing power shortages, by supplying high-quality biodiesel for diesel generators and industrial use.

About Us

PureGreenEnergies is a partnership under a group of investment companies, dedicated to manufacturing and selling biodiesel while also collecting used cooking oil. The company aims to provide a cheaper, more sustainable, and eco-friendly alternative to traditional diesel.

Our primary market includes the mining sector, hospitals, restaurants, and businesses that rely on diesel-powered generators. With Africa’s ongoing power supply challenges, our long-term vision is to expand biodiesel distribution across the continent, providing a stable energy solution.

PureGreenEnergies differentiates itself with competitive pricing, sustainability certifications, and strong partnerships with industries and restaurants.
